Recipe for Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, also called lasagne, is a type of pasta, probably one of the oldest, made in very wide, flat sheets. The Italian dish of the same name is made with stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheese, and seasonings and spices.
